About Me

I am currently a Ph.D candidate at the CIS department, the University of Melbourne, under the supervision of Prof. James Bailey, Dr. Sarah M. Erfani and Dr. Vinh Nguyen.

Prior to start my Ph.D, I received my Master degree in Distributed Computing from the University of Melbourne in 2014, and my Bachelor of Engineering degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology, China, in 2012.

I work in the area of data mining and machine learning, and my recent research focuses on tensor factorization approaches for big data.
